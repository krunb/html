WHAT IS DIVERSITY

Simply defined, diversity is the state of being diverse or different. Human diversity exists where there are differences in human characteristics such as gender, age, ethnicity and race or nationality, religion, social status and degree, sexual orientation, disability and health, or any other differentiating physical characteristics.
